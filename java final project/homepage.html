Mumbai the city that never sleeps

Mumbai, also known as the "City of Dreams", is India's financial, commercial, and entertainment capital. Famous for its bustling lifestyle, iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and Bollywood, it attracts millions of tourists every year. From historic architecture to modern skyscrapers, beaches to street food, Mumbai has a unique charm that blends tradition with modernity.
